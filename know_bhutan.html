Know Bhutan

Bhutan, the Kingdom of Happiness, is nestled in the Himalayas. Known for its rich culture, breathtaking landscapes, and the philosophy of Gross National Happiness.

Quick Facts

Bhutan is a small landlocked country in South Asia, located in the Eastern Himalayas.

Bhutan has a diverse landscape, from subtropical plains to high Himalayan peaks.

The capital of Bhutan is Thimphu, a city rich in tradition and modern culture.
